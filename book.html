Five simple steps.

From signup to highlight reel — exactly what to expect on your first visit.

01

Create account

Quick signup on CourtReserve unlocks live court availability and member rates.

02

Book a slot

Pick your sport, location, and time. Pay per play or apply your membership discount.

03

Let yourself in

Use the PIN from your reservation email to unlock the door — no front-desk wait.

04

Play

Show up, grab a racquet if you need one, and play. House paddles and shuttles included.
